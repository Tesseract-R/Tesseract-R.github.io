Seminar第1894期 On the planar dual Minkowski problem

报告主题：On the planar dual Minkowski problem
报告人：陈世炳 教授 （中国科学技术大学）
报告时间：2019年7月3日（周三）15:00
报告地点：校本部G507
邀请人： 席东盟
主办部门：理学院数学系
报告摘要： In this talk, we will discuss our solution to the planar dual Minkowski problem for all indices without symmetry assumption. The problem was originally proposed by Huang etc in their well known ACTA paper for the dual Minkowski problem. We use the PDE approach, in particular the Leray-Schauder degree theory, to tackle the problem. This is a joint work with Qirui Li.


欢迎教师、学生参加！
